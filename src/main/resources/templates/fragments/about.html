About Me

Software Developer | Automation Engineer | AI Enthusiast

I'm Bhavesh Dangi, a final-year Computer Engineering student at Presidency University, Bangalore, graduating in May 2025 with a minor in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CGPA: 8.01). I’m passionate about building impactful digital solutions that combine clean code, scalability, and innovation.

Currently, I work as an Automation Engineer at LG Soft India, designing large-scale automation frameworks for Smart TVs with CI/CD pipelines, Docker, MySQL-driven schedulers, and LLM-powered visual validation to accelerate testing efficiency and boost release velocity.

Previously, as an AI Software Intern at Soven, I engineered NLP pipelines using transformer models (BERT, Attention) to automate requirement analysis and dependency mapping, improving project planning efficiency significantly.

Beyond work, I’ve built full-stack projects like AgriDirect, a farmer-to-buyer marketplace, and Wanderlust, a hotel booking platform with AI chatbot integration. These projects reflect my ability to integrate ReactJS, Spring Boot, MySQL/PostgreSQL, and cloud platforms like AWS to deliver real-world solutions.

I’m also a problem solver, having solved 300+ LeetCode and 500+ DSA problems, and a continuous learner with a Google Cloud Generative AI Certification. My mission is to grow as a developer while creating technology that empowers people and drives innovation.
